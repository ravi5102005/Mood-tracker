Features

Description:


Record and remove your daily mood with ease. Select specific dates and get a clear overview of your month with a simple pie chart. Keep it simple, track your feelings, and gain insights into your emotional well-being.

Certainly! Here are the definitions for each feature:


Record or Remove Mood for the Day:


This feature allows users to log or delete their current emotional state for a specific day. It provides a way to keep a record of how they feel at any given moment.


Select Dates:


With this feature, users can choose specific dates to view or log their mood entries. It offers flexibility in tracking emotions for past, present, or future dates.


See Your Stats for the Month in the Form of a Pie Chart:


This feature provides users with a visual representation of their mood data for an entire month. It displays the distribution of different emotions in the form of a pie chart, offering a quick and easy-to-understand overview of their emotional patterns.
